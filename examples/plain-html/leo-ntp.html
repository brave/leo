Browse with me
About the Leo profile
This isolated Brave profile keeps your data separate from your main profile. Leo AI has extra permissions here to visit and interact with sites, so you can try giving it tasks and browsing together to boost productivity.
Your data and sessions
When you log into a website using this profile, Leo can see the information on that site if it's open, either when it visits the site itself or when you ask it to do something there.
Switching back
You can switch back to your regular profile at any time from the main menu, the Leo avatar in your toolbar, or clicking here. The Browse with me feature will always open in this profile.
